Intresting facts about Camels

Origami teddy-bear

Instructions to make a teddy bears
1 Fold down in half and unfold.

2 Fold the top and bottom edges to the center, then unfold.

3 Fold the left half to the right, then unfold.

4 Fold the left and right edges to the center, then unfold.

5 Fold top left corner to bottom right corner, then unfold.
